## Your First Visit

When you open Bonnie Wee Plot, you'll see the **Today** dashboard showing:
- What needs attention in your garden today
- Upcoming tasks based on your plantings
- Quick actions to add new plants

> **Tip:** Bonnie Wee Plot saves everything in your browser. No account needed - your data stays on your device.

### The Main Sections

| Section | Purpose |
|---------|---------|
| **Today** | Your daily dashboard - what needs doing now |
| **This Month** | Seasonal calendar - what to sow, transplant, harvest |
| **Seeds** | Seed inventory - track what you have and order |

## Adding Your First Planting

Recording plantings helps you track what's in each bed and when to expect harvests.

### Steps

1. Go to **Seeds** or **Today**
2. Click **Add Planting** (or tap the + button on mobile)
3. Choose your plant from the catalogue
4. Select which bed or area
5. Add optional details:
   - Variety name (e.g., "San Marzano")
   - Sow date
   - How you started it (indoor sow, outdoor sow, purchased transplant)

### What Gets Calculated

Once you add a planting with a sow date, Bonnie Wee Plot automatically calculates:
- Expected transplant date (if applicable)
- Estimated harvest window
- Days until harvest

All dates are adjusted for Scottish growing conditions.

## Tracking Seeds

The **Seeds** page helps you manage your seed inventory across seasons.

### Seed Status Options

For each variety, track whether you:
- **Have** seeds ready to sow
- **Ordered** but waiting for delivery
- **Had** but need to reorder

### Why Track Seeds?

- Know at a glance what you can sow now
- Plan ahead by marking varieties you want to try
- Build a history of what varieties worked well

## Using the Calendar

**This Month** shows personalised guidance based on:
- Your location (Scotland-specific timing)
- What you've already planted
- Seasonal sowing windows

### Understanding the Calendar

- **Sow indoors**: Start seeds under cover (windowsill, greenhouse)
- **Sow outdoors**: Direct sow in prepared beds
- **Transplant**: Move seedlings to final positions
- **Harvest**: Expected picking time

## Asking Aitor

Aitor is your AI gardening advisor, powered by OpenAI.

### Getting Started with Aitor

1. Go to **AI Advisor**
2. Enter your OpenAI API key (you only need to do this once per session)
3. Ask anything about gardening!

### Example Questions

> "When should I sow broad beans in Glasgow?"

> "My kale leaves have holes in them - what could be causing this?"

> "What grows well after potatoes?"

> "What should I plant in a shady corner?"

### Tips for Better Answers

- Mention your location (Aitor knows Scottish conditions)
- Share what you've already planted
- Include photos of problems for diagnosis

## Mobile Use

Bonnie Wee Plot works great on your phone - perfect for checking tasks while at the allotment.

### Install as an App

On iPhone or Android, you can add Bonnie Wee Plot to your home screen:

**iPhone:**
1. Open in Safari
2. Tap the Share button
3. Choose "Add to Home Screen"

**Android:**
1. Open in Chrome
2. Tap the menu (three dots)
3. Choose "Add to Home Screen"

This gives you an app-like experience with quick access from your phone.
